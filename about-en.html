🐹 About Mole Tap!

mole.dobal-e.com

What is this site?

Mole Tap! (mole.dobal-e.com) is a free whack-a-mole game that runs directly in your browser — no install, no sign-up. It was designed from scratch for mobile touch, aiming to recreate the feel of 1970s arcade machines on the web. Korean and English are supported.

Game features

  • 5 mole types including golden, helmet, bombs (3 designs) and bosses (5 designs), plus a combo & fever system
  • 30-second Classic mode and lives-based Survival mode
  • Haptic vibration and real-time synthesized sound effects and music
  • Automatic dark mode, add-to-home-screen (PWA) support

Technology & privacy

This is a static website built only with open web standards (HTML/CSS/JavaScript), served worldwide via Cloudflare. There are no accounts; your scores and settings are stored only on your device (browser local storage). See the Privacy Policy for details.

About ads

Mole Tap! is free to play and funded by ads (Google AdSense). Our rule is to place ads only where they never interfere with gameplay.
