Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026 · Mole Tap! (mole.dobal-e.com)

Mole Tap! (the "Site") values your privacy. This policy explains what information the Site handles and how.

1. Information we collect

The Site has no user accounts and does not collect or store personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.) on any server.

The following is stored only in your device's browser local storage and is never transmitted to the Site operator:

  • Best scores, selected difficulty and mode
  • Language (Korean/English) and sound on/off settings
  • Whether the cookie notice has been acknowledged

You can delete this data at any time using your browser's site-data controls.

2. Cookies and advertising

The Site serves ads through Google AdSense. In that process Google and its partners (third-party ad vendors) may use cookies.

  • Google may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • For details on Google's use of advertising data, see Google's advertising policies.
  • For users in regions requiring consent (EEA, UK, etc.), consent procedures under applicable regulations (e.g., GDPR) apply.

3. Hosting and access logs

The Site is delivered via Cloudflare. In providing the service and security (e.g., DDoS protection), Cloudflare may temporarily process ordinary connection information such as IP addresses. See the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

4. Children's privacy

The Site offers an all-ages game and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from any user, including children.

5. Third parties and international transfer

Since the Site operator holds no personal information, it does not sell or provide such information to third parties. Information may be processed abroad by Google and Cloudflare under their own policies as described in sections 2 and 3.

6. Changes to this policy

Changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated for material changes.
